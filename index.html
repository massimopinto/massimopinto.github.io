Hi, my name is Massimo Pinto and I am a research scientist working in the field of radiation dosimetry.

I see myself as an experimental physicist, but nowadays even experimental physics won't go too far unless one does some Monte Carlo simulations, and I am accordingly a sort of a chimera between a programmer and a code user. I live in Rome, Italy and I work at the the Italian National Institute for Metrology of Ionizing Radiation.

I am also very much into leisure cycling, be this for commuting or for going around with friends on a mountain bike in the many woods that surround the Rome region. I am a regular listener of (national public) radio programmes, which is making me feel like I belong to another era, sometimes.

And I am curious about the web and the publication opportunities that it provides. I have been a podcaster for several years, and a blogger for many more, publishing on free blogs such as Fisici per il mondo or hosted on the Italian science magazine Galileo. In fact, blogging and podcasting landed my on Nature a couple of times, while this hasn't happened for my research yet 8-).
